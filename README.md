# 📊E-Commerce-Analysis-Report
### **Data Source**
- CSV Files Provided: products.csv, orders.csv, order_items.csv, customers.csv, payments.csv
- Visualization Tool: Power BI
- Dataset Context: Global E-Commerce transactional data covering product categories, customer behavior, and payment methods.

### **Goals**
- To evaluate overall business performance across **sales, products, payments, and customer behavior**.  
- To identify **top-performing categories, products, and customers**.  
- To analyze **payment method efficiency** and transaction success rates.  
- To track **new vs. repeat customer trends** for retention and growth strategies.  

### **Outcomes**
- **Store Performance:**  
  - Total Revenue: **25.41M**  
  - Total Orders: **50K**  
  - Total Customers: **10K**  
  - Total Products: **1000**  
  - Total Stock Quantity: **505K**

- **Product Insights:**  
  - Leading categories: **Books (21.32%)**, Clothing (20.78%), Home & Kitchen (20.67%).  
  - Highest stock: **Clothing (110K units)**.  
  - Top products (e.g., Product_929, Product_305) generated sales above **69K** each.

- **Payment Analysis:**  
  - Payment methods are evenly distributed (~25% each).  
  - Success rate: **33.74%**, with failures and pending transactions nearly equal.  
  - Net Banking contributed the highest transaction amount (**6.43M**).

- **Customer Insights:**  
  - New Customers: **9942**  
  - Repeat Customers: **9551** (showing strong retention).  
  - Top customers contributed significantly, with the highest individual spending ~**8.8K**.  
  - Trend: **New customers declining, repeat customers increasing** — indicating loyalty growth.

### **Conclusions**
- The business demonstrates **balanced category performance**, with Books slightly leading in sales.  
- **Customer retention is improving**, as repeat customers are steadily rising, which is crucial for long-term sustainability.  
- **Payment systems need optimization** — nearly one-third of transactions fail or remain pending, which could impact customer trust.  
- Inventory is well-distributed, but **Clothing and Books dominate stock levels**, suggesting potential overstock risks if demand shifts.  
- Overall, the dashboards highlight a **healthy revenue stream, strong repeat customer base, and diverse product performance**, but also reveal opportunities to improve **payment success rates and inventory management**.
